The Ecosystem

Above: Original payment dashboard component

The visual design work spanned three primary touchpoints within Florida Blue’s multimodal AI support ecosystem, optimizing how data and conversations flowed across channels: Sunny, a member-facing health plan chatbot; AdvocateBot, an internal assistant for customer advocates; and VIVR, a mobile visual interface paired with phone-based service.

Design considerations

  • Maintain consistency with the existing design system and component library
  • Surface critical billing information in stategic payment management locations
  • Improve user journey scannability of balances, payment status, and actions
  • Ensure designs aligned with accessibility, state, and engineering constraints
Result

Introducing a payment snapshot component in the dashboard hero area allowed members to immediately see their current balance and access the payment center without navigating deeper into the application.

Reflection

Designing the payments experience reinforced how critical clarity and consistency are when users manage important tasks like insurance coverage and billing. When user interfaces clearly communicate status, next steps, and confirmation states, members are able to complete transactions with greater confidence and trust in the platform.

“Clear, system-driven interface design can turn complex account management tasks into confident, everyday actions.”
